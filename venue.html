Getting there


Mode Directions
Car
Parking: There is ample parking available on-site at Coltsford Mill for all guests.
Directions from the M25:
  1. From the M25, take Exit 6 (Godstone)
  2. At the roundabout, take the A22 exit toward East Grinstead/Caterham
  3. Follow signs for Oxted/Hurst Green, and continue onto A25
  4. Turn right onto Woodhurts Lane, then left onto+ Mill Lane
  5. Coltsford Mill will be on your right
GPS navigation may provide the most accurate directions based on current traffic conditions.
Train
Trains to Hurst Green Station:
  • Nearest station to Coltsford Mill is Hurst Green Station
  • Regular trains run from London Victoria
  • Approximately a 15 minute walk or a short taxi ride to Coltsford Mill
Walking from Hurst Green Station:
  1. Exist Hurst Green Station onto Hurstlands
  2. Turn right onto Pollards Oak Road
  3. Continue onto Mill Lane
  4. Follow Mill Lane until you reach Coltsford Mill on thr right
We recommend comfortable shoes if you choose to walk, as parts of Mill Lane may not have sidewalks.
Taxis
Local taxi services
  • Oxted Taxis: 01883 776000
  • Station Cars: 01883 723332
  • Metro Cars: 01883 345151
Taxis may be scarce especially at the end of the night. We highly recommend booking your return taxi in advance.

Accessibility

  • Coltsford Mill is fully wheelchair accessible and there are ramps and an accessible restroom available
  • If you have any other accessibility requirements, please let us know and we'd be happy to try and help :)

End of the night

  • The event concludes at midnight
  • Please ensure your transportation is arrange ahead of time.
